## The grounding that matters (so tomorrow doesn't re-derive it)

### Vanilla sleep prerequisite (decomp `Bed.Interact`, assembly_valheim ~:99592)
Five gates, ALL must pass: (1) time afternoon/night (`EnvMan.CanSleep`), (2) no enemies
(`!IsSensed`), (3) `CheckExposure` on the spawn point = `underRoof` AND cover ≥ 0.8, (4)
`CheckFire` = inside a burning Heat `EffectArea`, (5) not Wet. Night-skip itself is the
`AttachStart(isBed:true)` → `s_inBed` ZDO → `Game.EverybodyIsTryingToSleep` → `EnvMan.SkipToMorning`
chain; on wake `SetSleeping(false)` grants `SE_Rested` (so "comfort" is free, same event).
"Skip-only no spawn" = run that chain WITHOUT `SetCustomSpawnPoint` (impl-VERIFY flagged).

### Why the tent is visual-only shelter (Cover, assembly_utils)
Shelter = `Cover.GetCoverForPoint`: a 17-ray check (1 up, 8 diag-up, **8 horizontal**) needing
≥0.8, AND `IsUnderRoof` (upward spherecast hits a non-`leaky` collider on the cover mask
`{Default, static_solid, Default_small, piece, terrain, vehicle}`). The TraderTent canopy is
on `static_solid` and passes `underRoof` (→ keeps player dry, keeps a camp fire lit in rain),
but it's open-sided so it tops out ~0.47 cover — never 0.8. **So a vanilla bed under it fails
ONLY the 0.8 clause**; the special bedroll's prefab-gated `Bed.CheckExposure` relax (a later
card) is what makes sleep legal. ⚠️ This CORRECTS the old PARKED-2026-06-03 line "tents provide
own shelter status" — they don't, by the decomp.

### Why TraderTent needs a custom bundle (not graft-by-name)
`ZNetScene.GetPrefab` is a plain dict lookup over two curated serialized lists built at Awake —
NO SoftRef/Addressables fallback (decomp `:69111-69121`). TraderTent is lazy SoftRef location
decoration, NOT in those lists → `GetPrefab("TraderTent")` returns null (confirmed absent in
Jotunn's `prefab-list.md`; all known buildable donors present). So the Surveyor's-Table
graft-by-name pattern can't reach it. Bundle pipeline proven + saved as skill reference
`valheim-mod-development/references/custom-assetbundle-from-vanilla-mesh.md`.
