# ADR-NNNN: <short decision title>

- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-XXXX
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** <who>// e.g. Daniel + Starbright

## Context

What forces are at play? What problem or question prompted this decision?
Keep it to what a future reader (human or agent) needs to understand *why*.

## Decision

The change we are making, stated as a clear imperative. One paragraph.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or constrained as a result. Include the
"you must not undo this without a new ADR" line where the decision is
load-bearing.

## Alternatives considered

Briefly, what else was on the table and why it lost. This is the part that
stops the decision being re-litigated later.
